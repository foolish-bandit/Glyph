GLYPH

Legal symbol picker. Click any text field to see the Glyph icon, then click it to open the symbol panel.

Keyboard Shortcut
Ctrl + Shift + G
How to use: Focus on any text field → click the Glyph icon that appears → click a symbol to insert it. Your recently used symbols appear at the top.
